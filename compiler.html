Morpher: Best-in-Class CGRA Compiler

Our Morpher compiler is the best in class in the world for generating quality mapping, achieving fast compilation time, offering flexibility to explore different architectural designs, and providing end-to-end design flow.

Open Source Commitment

Made available as open-source in December 2022, Morpher has attracted collaborations from various research groups across the world. The framework enables rapid prototyping and benchmarking of CGRA architectures through standardized interfaces.
