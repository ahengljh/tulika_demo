Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the Morpher compiler?

Morpher is available as an open-source project on GitHub at github.com/ecolab-nus/morpher. You can clone the repository and follow the documentation to get started.

Can I use PACE chips in my research?

For academic research collaborations involving PACE hardware, please contact us directly at tulika@comp.nus.edu.sg to discuss possibilities for accessing development boards or FPGA emulation platforms.

Do you offer internship opportunities?

Yes, we occasionally offer research internships for outstanding undergraduate and graduate students. Please send your CV and a brief research statement to inquire about current openings.

How can I cite your work?

Please refer to our publications page for specific papers related to your work. Each publication includes citation information and links to the official sources.
